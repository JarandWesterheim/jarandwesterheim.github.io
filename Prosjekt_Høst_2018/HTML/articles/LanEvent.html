RAINBOW SIX PRO LEAGUE RETURNS TO BRAZIL

In November 2017, São Paulo hosted the Pro League Season 6 Finals. The event was a milestone, with 2,000 tickets selling out in less than two hours. During the event, fans saw hometown favorites Black Dragons take down the mighty PENTA Sports to reach the grand final. While they fought hard, in the end, the title went to the Finnish team ENCE. However, the energy of the Brazilian public was overwhelming, aiding the decision to return to Brazil to with the Season 8 Finals in November – in an arena ten times bigger – with the goal of serving the largest number of Rainbow Six fans in Brazilian esports history.
“It is a great honor to receive a Rainbow Six event of such relevance. This shows the importance of Brazil, with this final of 24,000 people, and places us as a true power in the esports market. We organized, together with ESL, the biggest Rainbow Six Siege event ever held in the word!”, says Bertrand Chaverot, managing director of Ubisoft Latin America. With a newly crowned Brazilian champion, an updated Pro League program, and exciting in-game changes, this season is gearing up to the best yet. We hope to see you all at the finals in November, and until then, you can keep up with all the Pro League action on our official Twitter and Twitch channels.
